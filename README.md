## Overview
The `NEMESIS-Theory` repository is the central archive for the mathematical frameworks, hypercomplex geometric proofs, and advanced theoretical physics papers developed by Mark at Nemesis 3D Studio. 

This corpus establishes the foundational rulesets for high-dimensional scalar geometries, quantum torsion, and multi-metric object spaces. It serves both as a standalone theoretical archive and as the geometric logic engine for the **UOR Foundation Framework**.

## Core Architectures

### 1. The Octeract Cypher & N-Sigmatics
A geometric model and ancient language decipherment framework based on 256 discrete states. 
* Utilizes 8-bit feature vectors to map complex semantic and physical states.
* Acts as the structural foundation for Octonian Braille and Moai Corpus mapping.
* Defines the associative and non-associative boundaries of 8-dimensional space.

### 2. The Cayley-Dickson Ladder (64D - 256D)
Operational logs and proofs detailing the binding and navigation of high-dimensional spaces using the Cayley-Dickson construction.
* Maps the transition from octonions to higher-order hypercomplex algebras.
* Establishes the mechanics of "7 Rings Navigation" and Octave Periodicity.

### 3. Nemesis Square & Nemesis Hyper Matrix
Multiplicative magic structures generated via the Golden Ratio exponentiation of prime sequences.
* Provides the routing tables and structural arrays for symmetric object spaces.
* Serves as the mathematical backbone for calculating void-to-manifestation interactions (Negative Solids).

### 4. Torsion Capacitance & Neo Vortex Equations
Explorations into the physical resistance of state-changes within non-associative trajectories.
* Includes toy models for Torsion Domain-Wall Capacitors.
* Correlates with Garcia de Andrade’s Torsion Potentials.
* Formalizes the Sage Vortex and Neo Vortex energetic equations.

## UOR Foundation Resonance Map
The mathematics housed in this repository are mathematically locked in resonance with the **[UOR-Framework](https://github.com/UOR-Foundation/UOR-Framework)**, a Rust-based ontology for symmetric, multi-metric object spaces.

The UOR kernel computes relationships using modular arithmetic defined by $Z/(2^n)Z$. Nemesis Theory provides the hypercomplex geometric mapping for these spaces, specifically achieving absolute resonance at $n = 8$ (a 256-dimensional space, mirroring the Octeract). 

Documents such as the `N-Sigmatics Json` act as the serialization blueprint to convert Nemesis geometries into machine-readable JSON-LD, seamlessly feeding into the UOR `uor-ontology` and passing through the `uor-conformance` validation suite.

## Applied Mechanics
While fundamentally theoretical, these frameworks power several applied engines and prototypes, including:
* **Fano Plane DOOM:** Sound and chord generation protocols based on 7-point geometric symmetries.
* **Nemesis Core Cosmology:** The underlying reality-engine for miniature-agnostic tabletop simulations.
